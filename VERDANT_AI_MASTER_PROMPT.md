# 🌱 Verdant AI — PromptWars Challenge 3

## Project Overview

You are a Senior Product Architect, Staff Software Engineer, UI/UX Designer, AI Engineer, Cloud Architect, and Startup Founder.

Your task is to design and build a production-ready AI-powered sustainability platform called **Verdant AI**.

---

# Challenge Statement

Design a solution that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple actions and personalized insights.

---

# Product Vision

Most carbon footprint applications only calculate emissions.

Verdant AI should act as an **AI Sustainability Operating System** that:

- Understands user lifestyle
- Tracks environmental impact
- Identifies emission hotspots
- Predicts future outcomes
- Recommends personalized improvements
- Motivates users through gamification
- Makes sustainability engaging and rewarding

---

# Product Tagline

**"Turn Everyday Choices Into Climate Impact."**

Alternative:

**"Your Personal AI Sustainability Coach."**

# Feature 1 — Carbon DNA™

## Purpose

Create a sustainability profile for every user.

## Inputs

### Transportation

- Car usage
- Bike usage
- Public transport
- Flights
- Ride-sharing

### Home Energy

- Electricity consumption
- Renewable energy usage
- AC usage
- Appliance usage

### Food Habits

- Vegetarian
- Vegan
- Mixed diet
- Meat consumption frequency

### Shopping Habits

- Fashion purchases
- Electronics purchases
- Online shopping

### Waste Management

- Recycling habits
- Plastic usage
- Composting habits

## Outputs

- Carbon Footprint Estimate
- Sustainability Score
- Lifestyle Classification
- Environmental Impact Summary

Example:

"You are an Urban Commuter with moderate transport emissions and above-average energy consumption."
